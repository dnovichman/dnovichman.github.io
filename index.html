Welcome of Rokel Delivery Services

Rokel Delivery Services (RDS) is a small startup company aimed at producing a fully autonomous aerial delivery system for medical products in remote/rural areas and in congested cities.

We want to connect services and products to all people in the quickest possible time with the belief that the geographic location of someone cannot determine whether they receive one ounce of an available life-saving drug in a timely manner.
